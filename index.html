Notes

We buy non-peforming notes with the goal of keeping borrowers in their homes and achieving reperformance. We incorporate a third-party credit counselor in our loss mitigation strategy that has enabled us to achieve better than industry average results.

Fix and Flips

In specific markets we have construction crews in place that have enabled us to engage in light to heavy rehab projects. Once the properties are fixed up we have several exit strategies including: selling on the open market, seller finance, lease option, and keeping as a long term rental

account_balance

Seller Finance and Lease Options

Our favorite exit strategy is creating seller financed notes and lease options. These are great cash flowing investments and an excellent way to achieve passive income. Often times our borrowers will refinance our notes with convential lending, providing us higher than expected returns.
